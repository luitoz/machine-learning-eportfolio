The so-called “smartization” of industries, often referred to as Industry 4.0, represents a paradigm shift driven by artificial intelligence, automation, and machine learning. With the rapid advancement of AI and ML, we are moving towards a world where self-learning systems, efficient AI architectures, and edge computing are becoming the backbone of modern technological ecosystems.

Machine learning is evolving beyond traditional supervised learning, with Self-Supervised Learning (SSL) unlocking AI’s ability to learn from unlabelled data, Edge AI enabling real-time decision-making on low-power devices, and Neural Architecture Search (NAS) automating deep learning model design. These future trends are shaping the next era of AI-powered solutions across healthcare, finance, autonomous systems, and smart cities.

## Learning outcomes
- Understand the challenges and opportunities of industry 4.0 revolution.
- Appreciate the impact of machine learning in future societies.
