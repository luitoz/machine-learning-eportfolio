Machine Learning (ML) has become an integral part of our daily life. From browsing on internet through to getting a bank loan, ML is used to make decisions and for profiling. The availability of big data on a real-time basis facilitates the learning of the ML algorithms, which in turn provides machines with the ability to make more accurate decisions autonomously (Schwab & Zahid 2020).

## Learning outcomes
- Have a better understanding of the of role machine learning in future industry.
- Identify skill sets required to become proficient in machine learning.
- Know about the pitfalls of machine learning and ways to address it.
